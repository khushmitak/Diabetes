10 Fun Facts About Washington State

  • Washington is named after George Washington, the first President of the United States.
  • The Boeing Everett Factory, world's largest building by volume is in Washington state.
  • Washington state has four of the five longest floating bridges in the world.
  • Washington state is home to Rainier cherries.
  • Washington state is the leading producer of apples in the United States.
  • Washington state is known for mining metals such as aluminum, lead, and gold.
  • Seattle in Washington is known as "Emerald City" because of the evergeen trees all year long.
  • Pickleball was invented in Washington.
  • An entire fake town was built in Washington to hide an airplane factory.
  • There is a bicyle growing in a tree on Vashon Island.
